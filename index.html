Event Guest List Manager

This application provides a simple and user-friendly interface
that allows users to manage a guest list with ease. Users can add
guest names to the list and remove them at any time, making it
ideal for events, parties, or any occasion that requires guest tracking.
With its straightforward design and functionality, this app ensures a
hassle-free experience in keeping your guest list organized and up to date.
